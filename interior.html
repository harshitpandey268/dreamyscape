Consultation

We begin by understanding your preferences, needs, and budget. Our experts will conduct a thorough assessment of your space, discuss your design goals, and offer valuable insights and recommendations.

Concept Development

Our designers will develop a unique concept that aligns with your style and requirements. From creating mood boards to selecting color palettes, furniture, and materials, we'll craft a cohesive vision for your space.

Space Planning

We'll optimize the layout of your space to maximize functionality and flow. Our team will create detailed floor plans, ensuring that every inch is utilized effectively and that the design caters to your specific needs.

Furniture and Accessories Selection

We'll help you choose the perfect furniture, lighting fixtures, window treatments, and accessories that complement your design concept. From sourcing unique pieces to coordinating deliveries, we'll take care of all the logistics.

Material and Finishes Selection

Our experts will guide you through a wide range of materials, finishes, and textures for surfaces such as flooring, walls, countertops, and cabinetry. We'll assist you in selecting high-quality options that enhance the aesthetic appeal of your space.

Project Management

Dreamyscape will oversee the entire design process, from procurement to installation. We'll collaborate with contractors, craftsmen, and suppliers to ensure seamless execution and timely completion of your project

Styling and Finishing Touches

Our attention to detail extends to the final touches that bring your design to life. We'll curate artwork, arrange decor items, and style your space to create a harmonious and inviting atmosphere.
